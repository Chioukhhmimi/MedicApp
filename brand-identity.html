Brand Identity

Dosely

A warm, trustworthy medicine & pill reminder app that helps people stay on schedule — without the clinical coldness.

01 — Logo

The Mark

A tilted capsule fused with a reminder clock. The pill says medicine; the clock badge says on time. Together they tell the whole story at a glance — even at app-icon size.

Logomark — app icon
Wordmark — reversed
02 — Meaning

Why it works

  • Capsule — the universal symbol for medication, instantly readable.
  • Clock badge — the reminder layer; turns "pill" into "pill, on time."
  • Two-tone split — the coral half adds warmth and a human touch to a health tool.
  • Rounded squircle — friendly and modern; conforms perfectly to iOS & Android icon masks.

05 — Usage

Do & Don't

Do

  • Keep clear space around the mark equal to the clock badge's diameter.
  • Use the gradient tile on light backgrounds; the reversed wordmark on dark/teal.
  • Reserve coral for primary actions and alerts only.
  • Maintain the capsule's −45° angle at all times.

Don't

  • Don't recolor the capsule or rotate it upright.
  • Don't add drop shadows or outlines to the wordmark.
  • Don't stretch, condense, or skew the logo.
  • Don't place the mark on busy photos without a solid tile.
